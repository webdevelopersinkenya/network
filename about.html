ABOUT US

OVERVIEW

Godiya Enterprise prides itself in being able to engineer and implement point-to-point and multi- point transmission networks across Kenya. These solutions – including aerial and underground fibre installations, as well as the many variants of fibre optic access infrastructure – facilitate the shift to digitisation, helping businesses adapt to the changing requirements of the digital age. We specialise in planning, designing, installing and maintaining structured cabling systems that are flexible and scalable to match our clients' requirements. Fuelled by a passionate commitment to our client relationships, we're persistent in finding the best-fit solutions and technology to ensure optimal business outcomes and the continued success of our clients. We constantly seek to improve our capabilities, capacity and resourcefulness that manifests in quality, consistent service and superior delivery satisfaction.

Godiya Enterprise Limited is an ICT infrastructure and telecoms company that provides structured cabling, fibre and wireless networks to public and private clients in Kenya. We provide integrated strategic corporate services and leadership through our professional services and diversified offerings.
